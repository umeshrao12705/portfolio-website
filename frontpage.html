Portfolio Management System

The \Portfolio Management System is a web-based application that enables students to create, manage, and update their personal portfolios. This system allows users to input their personal details, educational qualifications, and work experience, ensuring a well-organized record of their achievements.

Key Features:

  • Create Portfolio: Enter personal, academic, and work details easily.
  • View All Entries: Display student records in a structured format.
  • Edit/Delete Information: Update/Delete details whenever needed.
  • Data Persistence: Information remains saved even after closing the browser.
